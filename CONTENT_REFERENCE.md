# Content Reference from Existing Site

## Key Facts & Messaging

### Headline Facts
- **Oldest Youth Choir in South Africa** (founded 1967)
- **Founded by**: Mr. Hein de Villiers
- **Current Conductor**: Bernard Krüger (conductor, composer, accomplished musician)
- **Size**: ~80 choristers
- **Geographic Reach**: Greater Durban, KZN North and South Coast (Port Edward, Kokstad, Richards Bay, Mooi River, Newcastle)

### Repertoire
- Classical (sacred and secular)
- Pop choral
- Traditional African music
- Reflects cultural diversity

### Achievements
- **2019**: 13th Summa Cum Laude International Youth Music Festival (Vienna, Austria)
  - **1st place with outstanding success** in Mixed Choir category
  - Only choir representing African continent
  - Competed against choirs from Australia, China, Taiwan, Brazil
  - Tour included performances in Croatia and Slovenia

### Objectives
1. Build the choral tradition in KZN
2. Promote stylistically correct choral singing
3. Engage youth in worthwhile activity in an organisation based on Christian ethos and atmosphere of encouragement
4. Enrich the community through concerts and cultural development
